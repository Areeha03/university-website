Hero Academy



Description:

Japan's premier hero academy, training students with unique "Quirks" (superpowers) to become professional heroes through rigorous academics, specialized training, and a strict, yet nurturing, environment, known for its motto "Go Beyond, Plus Ultra,"
Curriculum: Combines standard high school subjects with practical hero training, including battles, first aid, and disaster relief simulations.
Facilities: Features state-of-the-art training grounds (Ground Beta, Gamma, Omega), the Unforeseen Simulation Joint (USJ), and secure on-campus dorms (Heights Alliance).
Culture: Known for its intense atmosphere, competitive spirit (especially during the UA Sports Festival), and high standards, with potential expulsion for underperformers.
